Sanjay Vaidya

A Glamour Photographer since 1985, Sanjay Vaidya, turns the spotlight on some of the most enduring icons of Gujarati Literature of our times, Dr. Gunvant Shah, Chandrakant Bakshi, Dr. Suresh Dalal, Ramesh Parekh, Vinod Bhatt, Tarak Mehta etc. through a chain of photo-books called ‘E-series’. He turned to publishing in 2001 and since then he has compiled and edited the most cherished account of its kind, along with their candid photographs ever published in the field of literature.

Sanjay grew up in a literary and intellectually stimulated environment and treated his academic career casually. In his search for a creative self, he often found himself standing at an intersection where his ideology can meet with his expression. Although being in a constant hustle-bustle with academics, he completed his graduation in English Literature & Post Graduation in Journalism. He began his professional journey with photo-journalism and fashion photography. After spending major years of his life in the glamour world, he moved to the quest for a life in literature and photography full time.
